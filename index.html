Rise Of Remote Working

Since the start of COVID people have been subjected to working from home, this was an effort to help productions and allow people
to provide for their families. But like with everything, as we adapt to make a living so does the efforts of the cyber criminals.
Studies show an increase of cyber attack have increased as we work remote, one article states in 2021 there has been a rise in
ransomware attacks from the CYBERSECURITY & INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY AGENCY ( NISA.GOV .)
This isn’t to scare anyone from using the internet or prevent them from working from home, it’s to inform the public to
Take precaution when working from home or in general browsing the internet.
